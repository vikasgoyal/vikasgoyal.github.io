# Agentic AI Practice Area Map

This map groups the article pages in `agentic` and `genai` into practical Agentic AI practice areas.

Scope:
- Included article and report pages in `agentic` and `genai`.
- Excluded shared partials and non-article files such as `header.html`, `footer.html`, `assets/*`, and folder landing pages like `index.html`.
- Each article is assigned one primary practice area, even when it also fits a secondary area.

## Practice Areas Used

1. **Strategy, Adoption, and Ecosystem Signals**: executive framing, market timing, maturity, and landscape tracking.
2. **Agent Foundations and Behavior Models**: core concepts such as agenticness, reasoning modes, task types, and the evolution of agent behavior.
3. **Architecture, Platforms, and Protocol Engineering**: reference architectures, enterprise system design, MCP, and implementation contracts.
4. **Multi-Agent Coordination and Orchestration**: MAS concepts, interaction patterns, and reference architectures for coordinated agents.
5. **Learning, Memory, and Personalization**: adaptation, teachability, memory, and user-specific behavior shaping.
6. **Evaluation, Reliability, and Interaction Robustness**: eval design, failure prevention, structured outputs, and resilient interaction handling.
7. **Work Design, Cognitive UX, and Human-AI Collaboration**: how people and agents divide work, reduce cognitive load, and co-create outcomes.
