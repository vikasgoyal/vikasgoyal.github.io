Privacy Policy

December 28, 2025

Your trust matters. This page explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit vikasgoyal.github.io.

Information We Collect

The site is primarily informational, yet limited data is gathered to keep traffic secure and understand what resonates with readers.

  • Information you provide directly: When you reach out via email or social channels linked across the site, the details you share are used to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage and diagnostics data: Aggregated analytics from services such as Google Analytics highlight page views, referring domains, and device types to improve content planning.
  • Cookies and local storage: Lightweight cookies may remember preferences (for example, dismissing banners) and support measurement scripts. You can clear or block them through your browser settings.

How We Use Information

Information is used solely for operating and improving the site, never for selling data or creating intrusive profiles.

  • Measure readership trends to prioritize topics across Agentic AI, GenAI, and startup content.
  • Ensure pages load reliably and detect abusive traffic patterns that could impact availability.
  • Respond to inbound requests for collaboration, speaking, or advisory work.

Any analytics insights are reviewed in aggregate; there is no interest in identifying individual visitors beyond responding to direct outreach.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Data is not sold or shared with advertisers. Limited third parties support site operations:

  • Hosting: GitHub Pages delivers the site globally and logs standard server metrics to maintain reliability.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies capture anonymous usage statistics. You can opt out with browser add-ons provided by Google.
  • Embedded services: Occasionally, articles embed third-party content (for example, YouTube or social posts). Interaction with those services is governed by their respective policies.

Access to any personal correspondence is limited to Vikas Goyal and trusted collaborators who assist with inbox triage under strict confidentiality.

Your Choices and Contact

You control how your data is used:

  • Update your browser preferences to block or delete cookies at any time.
  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on for deeper control.
  • Email vikas@vikasgoyal.com or connect via LinkedIn to request a summary of any direct communications or to ask for deletion.

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated modification date. Continued use of the site after changes indicates acceptance of the revised terms.
